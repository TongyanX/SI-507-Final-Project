To see GDP time trends (from 1997 to 2016) of all your interested states together. Better within 20 states.

To see discrimination of tuition and fees in public universities for in-state and out-of-state students.

Bubble plot - University vs GDP

To see relationship between university amount and GDP value. You can also add some other features.

Scatter plot - Public vs Private

To see differences between public and private universities. You can choose features whatever you like.

Map Plot - University Location

To see a map including all universities in your interested state (mapbox API key is required).
